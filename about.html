About Us

Nitya Chikankari is dedicated to preserving the timeless art of Lucknowi chikankari. Our artisans handcraft each saree, kurti, and suit with intricate embroidery, blending traditional techniques with contemporary designs. Rooted in the cultural heritage of Lucknow, we bring you elegant, sustainable fashion that celebrates craftsmanship and empowers local communities.
